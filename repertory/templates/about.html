Austin Rep Calendar

Oh, hi again.

Hi, I'm Zack McGhee. I made this thing. You can email me with corrections and/or complaints.

How it works

I curate the calendar listings. It's a rolling three-week calendar, updated weekly. Currently, the listings go through June 4.

The next update will be Tuesday, May 26 to update the listings through Thursday, June 11.

Current venues are restricted to Austin Film Society (at the Marchesa and the Screening Room), the Paramount, the Stateside (because, inexplicably, some folks I know still see movies there), and all Austin Alamo Drafthouse locations. That's it for now, although I may expand the list in the future.

Special Thanks

Special thanks to all the programmers and cinephiles who make Austin great.

Extra special thanks to Tommy Swenson and Chris Hvizdak for their help and inspiration in the creation of this service.

Top features on my mind

  1. You should be able to remove items when viewing your personal calendar
  2. You should be able to sync your calendar to your iCal or Google Calendar
  3. There should be a secret but publicly viewable URL for sharing your personal calendar (if you want to)
  4. There should be a cute little icon for when you add the calendar to your home screen
  5. You should be able to see what your Facebook friends are seeing (if they want you to)
  6. There should be running times, IMDb links, and ticket links when possible

The code for this is all open source, btw. Pull requests welcome.
