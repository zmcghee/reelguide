Austin Rep Calendar

Oh, hi again.

I'm Zack McGhee. I made this thing. You can email me with corrections and/or complaints.

How It Works

I curate the calendar listings. It's a rolling three-week calendar, updated weekly. Currently, the listings go through July 2.

The next update will be Monday, June 22 to update the listings through Thursday, July 9.

Current venues are restricted to Austin Film Society (at the Marchesa and the Screening Room), the Paramount and Stateside Theatres, the Bob Bullock and Texas Spirit Theater, and all Austin Alamo Drafthouse locations. That's it for now, although I may expand the list in the future.

Special Thanks

Special thanks to all the film programmers and cinephiles who make Austin movie culture great.

Extra special thanks to Tommy Swenson and Chris Hvizdak for their help and inspiration in the creation of this service.

Built on Open Source

Thanks also to all the communities of open source projects and their contributors that power this application. Python, Django, Redis, PostgreSQL, SQLite, jQuery, jQuery Mobile, Bootstrap, and several supporting libraries, as well as The Movie Database—which is not open source, but is a great, free, user-editable resource with a robust API—made it possible for version one of this app to go up after one all-night coding session, so thank you.

The code for this is all open source, btw. Pull requests welcome.

Top features on my mind
  1. You should be able to sync the public calendar or your personal calendar to your iCal or Google Calendar (this is in testing now)
  2. There should be a secret but publicly viewable URL for sharing your personal calendar (if you want to)
  3. There should be running times, IMDb links, and ticket links when possible
  4. You should be able to remove items while viewing your personal calendar
  5. You should be able to save a filter that excludes venues, series, and formats you don't care about
  6. You should be able to see what your Facebook friends are seeing (if they want you to)
